DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT PROCESS

01.Information Gathering

This first step is actually the most important one, as it involves a solid understanding of the company. I start off by asking a lot of questions to help me understand your business and your needs in a website.

Planning .02

Using the information i've gathered from phase one, I put together a plan for your website.
I will also help you decide which technologies I will implement.
The plan consists of of:

  • Main topics
  • Sub topics
  • Content

03. Design

I will create prototype designs for your website. I will also send an Adobe.PDF file of what the final design will look like for your approval.
I will communicate with you more closely, exchanging ideas until I match your needs and ideas.

Development .04

This is where I create an actual functional website.
Elements such as interactive forms, animations or e-commerce shopping carts are implemented.
I will also inform you of my progress so that you can suggest additional changes or collections.

05.Testing & Delivery

I will attend to the final details and test your website.I will make sure that all the code meets current web development standards.
Once you give mme your final approval, i'll run the website through a live server, then it is time to launch the site. Your website is now viewable to the public.

Maintenance .06

I will be more than happy to continue working together with you to update the information on your website.
